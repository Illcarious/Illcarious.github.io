I think I know how to

Make this thing a little more sustainable. I feel like I can; I might be in over my head, but I'm gonna try and do "one day builds" of the UIs of popular websites/webapps. I remembered this type of challenge when I was looking through the CSS Layout tutorial and the picture layout reminded me of Instagram. And I know that webdev isn't necessarily my goal, but I want to at least prove to myself that I have the self-discipline/sufficiency to learn the front-end stuff beyond the scope of coursework and hopefully develop the affluency for full stack.

For now, baby steps. And yes, I know there are lots of people who have absolutely no background in CS, quit their jobs and teach themselves how to make a web platform with 100K users in a month, I get that. But I'm learning at my own pace, and other things as well instead of just webdev.
