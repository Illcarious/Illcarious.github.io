Morning

I remember hearing from somewhere that you should do your most important things in the morning because this is the time when we have the most willpower. Also, willpower is something that gets drained throughout the day. That's why it gets harder and harder to do something you know you should do but don't want to do as you go through the day. I'm feeling the effects of that right now since it is a Sunday and I just wanna watch some Netflix. But I would be disappointing myself if I didn't get this thing done.

If you have time to complain, you have time to work/p>
