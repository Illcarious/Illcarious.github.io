Choices

Life is about choices. Learning from the bad ones and cherishing the good ones. I haven't had nearly as much experience on this planet as most people have so the choices I make can seem rather foolish. But isn't that how the saying goes? "You live and you learn"? The biggest decision I've had to make to date has been 'what career should I pursue'? And by that I mean, 'what degree should I learn'? There are so many factors at play when it comes time to choose your education-and ultimately-the career path you take. Long story short, after the first degree, I've decided to take responsibility for my own choices and also MAKE my own choices. If it meant disobeying my parents and creating short-term tension, then so be it, because I'm not willing to live with a resentment towards them due to listening to their wishes for what I should learn and become. I'm hoping that this is a long-term play, but only time will tell. For the time being, all I can do is control my own effort through the pursuit of this second degree.
